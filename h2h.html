English Football Head-to-Head Statistics

Tool for finding the head-to-head statistics between any two teams in the English Football League.

This page can be directly linked to by passing team names into the url, e.g. to get the head-to-head statistics of Arsenal v Chelsea visit: https://seanelvidge.com/h2h?team1=Arsenal&team2=Chelsea
